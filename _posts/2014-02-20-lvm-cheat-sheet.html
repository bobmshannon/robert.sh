I'm not very good at memorizing things. That's why I think it is a good idea to keep verbose information documented somewhere for easy access later on.

Creating Physical Volumes:

pvcreate /path/to/device

Creating Volume Groups:

vgcreate VOLGROUPNAME /path/to/physicalvolume1 /path/to/physical/volume2

Creating Logical Volumes:

lvcreate -L 5G VOLGROUPNAME

View Existing Physical Volumes:

pvdisplay

View Existing Volume Groups:

vgdisplay

Viewing Existing Logical Volumes:

lvdisplay
